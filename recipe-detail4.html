Healthy Salad

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and shredded carrots.
  2. Add diced avocado and sprinkle with feta cheese, if using.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar or lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your healthy salad!
